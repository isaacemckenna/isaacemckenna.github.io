Isaac McKenna

Photo Sculpture Design Video + More About

Isaac McKenna is a photographer, sculptor, and artist based in Seattle, WA. They graduated with honors in Visual Art from Brown University. Isaac's work spans a variety of photographic and sculptural mediums, and centers around questions of temporality, image-subject collapse, and the perspective of natural environments.

Isaac has exhibited work in solo and group shows at the RISD Museum's Gelman Gallery, the Granoff Center for the Creative Arts, and List Art Center, and has been published in The Indy and the Brown University Art Review.
