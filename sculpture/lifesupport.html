Life Support

Cast concrete, wire, chain, LED strip, sheet steel, circuit boards, slide image, enter key. 2023

A hanging monument to a slide image found in a secondhand store, "Life Support" considers how our consciousness and memory may live on through the images we produce. This is especially true of the digital age, and yet physical storage like the slide this sculpture is built around has a different set of preservative qualities.
