Impressions

Cast concrete. 2023

These concrete pillows were created using a physical-digital-physical process. Using reference images and 3D scans, I modeled my everyday items (glasses, phone, keys, and wallet), and performed cloth simulations to ‘impress’ them onto pillow models. 3D-printed molds brought the forms to the physical world in concrete, creating a sort of archeological evidence for my existence.
