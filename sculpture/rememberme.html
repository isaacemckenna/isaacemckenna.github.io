Remember Me

Cardboard, plaster strips. 2023

This piece translates meticulously 3D-modeled human teeth into oversized sculptures. For each tooth, a laser cut lattice is wrapped in plaster strips, resulting in a highly textural and misshapen surface. The piece is placed on the floor, and the set of teeth is incomplete, referencing the archeological significance of teeth as records of existence, and the simultaneous incompleteness of archeological methods.
