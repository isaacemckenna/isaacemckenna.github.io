I'm Isaac, a designer, artist, filmmaker, musician, and editor. Home is Boston, MA or Ann Arbor, MI, depending on the day. I am currently a Junior at Brown University double-majoring in Visual Art and Film. I have experience in print, digital, and experimental design, and love creative challenges.

I like to create art which explores memory, surveillance, and the intersection of art and technology—I am especially interested in using technology such as Adobe tools, Blender, Ableton, and analog platforms to create art. I am also committed to promoting progressive politics through my work as a newspaper editor, and am constantly thinking about how design and art can make the world a better place.

Please reach out to me if you are interested in working with me, have questions about my work, or just want to talk!
