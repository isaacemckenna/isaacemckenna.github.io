Isaac McKenna · Ode To The Providence/Stoughton Line

"Ode to the Providence/Stoughton Line — Isaac McKenna"

Experimental ambient track. Combination of field recordings and vocals programmed into MIDI. Created to describe the meditative effect of riding public transport from Providence to Boston.

All production: Isaac McKenna.

Isaac McKenna · Real & Unreal

"Real & Unreal — Isaac McKenna"

Two tracks built from the same field recordings. "Real" is representational, whereas "Unreal" abstracts the sounds from their sources through editing. Both tracks contain recordings from Providence and Boston.

All production: Isaac McKenna.
